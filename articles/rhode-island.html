Rhode Island Attractions and Local Culture

Rhode Island is known for its rich culture, stunning natural beauty, and strong local business community. Visitors and residents alike enjoy The Breakers, Cliff Walk Newport, and WaterFire Providence. The local food scene is famous for coffee milk and clam cakes — all deeply tied to the culture and history of the state.

Local businesses in Rhode Island thrive by serving both longtime residents and the thousands of visitors who come to experience everything the state has to offer. From small family-owned shops to established service companies, Rhode Island businesses are proud of their roots and their communities.
